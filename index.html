What Makes Us Unique

Life Vault offers a secure platform for Personally Identifiable Financial Information (PIFI), distinguishing itself in the realm of digital security.

Customers maintain control over their data privacy, empowered with an easy share function to selectively disclose information.

The platform provides an easy shareable time-sensitive link, enhancing the efficiency of data sharing while maintaining security protocols.

Life Vault ensures the secure storage of various digital assets including passwords, logins, phone codes, and more, offering comprehensive protection for users' digital lives.

Upon passing, Life Vault facilitates the release of an individual's digital life data to their next of kin, contingent upon the provision of a death certificate number, thus enabling a seamless transition of digital assets in accordance with the user's wishes.
