Welcome to the Speen
Community Cafe

We are a 'pop-up' cafe, kindly hosted by The Starting Gate pub, every Wednesday between 2-4pm. Run entirely by local volunteers, we serve homemade cakes, biscuits, tea, coffee and squash. We also bring board games, newspapers, colouring and crafts.

Community

Everyone is welcome at the community cafe. Please join us for cakes and other lovely treats. But mainly join us for community!!! We love chatting and seeing you all mingling. We are getting between 40-70 guests a week now, so come out of your comfort zone and engage with us. We have LOVELY people here. Every Wednesday 2-4pm @ The Starting Gate pub, Brummell Rd, Speen, Newbury.

In the Press!

Speen Community Cafe in the Newbury Weekly News. We made it in to the press with Chris Tarrant judging our Bake Off 2023!!


About Us

Kerry Bird the founder of the community cafe has been running it since 2021 along with a lovely group of volunteers. If you would like to help as a volunteer, please send us an email.
